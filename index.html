About

Squash & Stretch is a small indie game development studio hailing from Victoria, Australia.
The studio is currently deep into the development of its first commercial game 'Rat Trap'.

Passion with a purpose

Creating the kinds of games that we would want to play, confident others will love.

Retro meets modern

Utilising inspiration from not only the hits of today, but also from gaming's lucrative past.

Fun comes first

We strive to deliver authentic and fun-first experiences, without any bloat or fluff that can sometimes get in the way.

Humble beginnings

The studio began as a solo dev's passion project, and has since gained the aid of other talented creatives along the way.
